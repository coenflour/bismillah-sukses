SEVENTEEN

Say The Name! SEVENTEEN Right Here!

SEVENTEEN/ SVT(세븐틴) is a boy group from South Korea formed by Pledis Entertainment. SEVENTEEN consists of 13 members, namely S.Coups, Jeonghan, Joshua, Jun, Hoshi, Wonwoo, Woozi, The8, Mingyu, DK, Seungkwan, Vernon and Dino.

SEVENTEEN has 3 units, namely the vocal unit with leader Woozi, performance unit with leader Hoshi, and hip hop unit with leader S.Coups. There is also a BSS unit led by Boo Seungkwan, Lee Seokmin (DK) and Kwon Soonyoung (Hoshi).

Seventeen fans are called Carats and have official fandom colors, namely Rose Quartz and Serenity. For the latest information about Seventeen, fans can follow their social media accounts such as Instagram (@saythename_17), Twitter (@pledis_17), Facebook (seventeennews), vLive (Seventeen channel), and the official website (seventeen-17.com). There is also a Fan Cafe under the name Pledis-17 and a YouTube channel under the name Seventeen.

S.COUPS

LEADER

  • Full name: Choi Seungcheol
  • Stage Name: S.Coups
  • Place, date of birth: August 8, 1995 in Daegu, South Korea
  • Position in the group: Hip Hop Unit, Rapper
  • Nationality: Korean

S.Coups aka Choi Seungcheol is the first member of Seventeen who is also the leader and oldest member. Born on August 8, 1995 in Daegu, South Korea. In the Seventeen group, S.Coups has the position of leader of the Hip-Hop team, rapper, and sub-vocalist. His stage name, S.Coups, comes from 'S' which is taken from "Sungcheol" and 'Coups' comes from "Coup d'etat".

S.Coups has been a trainee since 2010 and even almost debuted with the boy group NU'EST. Not only that, S.Coups was also the initial member of the boy group TEMPEST', before Seventeen was created. You could say, S.Coups is an original member of the Pledis Entertainment trainees aka Pledis Boys. Therefore, he really knows his seniors at Pledis Entertainment, starting from NU'EST and After School.

JUN

DANCER

  • Full name: Wen Junhui
  • Stage Name: Jun
  • Place, date of birth: June 10, 1996 in Guangdong, China
  • Position in the group: Performance Unit, Dancer
  • Nationality: Chinese

Jun aka Wen Junhui was born on June 10, 1996 in Guangdong, China. His Korean name is Wen Junhui. In the group, Jun plays as lead dancer and sub-vocalist. Jun's face looks similar to Super Junior's Heechul and Heechul even admitted this himself. Just like most students in China who like K-Pop, Jun did the same and decided to move to Korea so he could finally make his debut like he is today.

Before moving to Korea, Jun was a child actor and appeared in the legendary film The Legend is Born - Ip Man, as the young IP Man. Therefore, Jun is good at Wushu and Kung Fu martial arts. He can also speak 3 languages, namely Mandarin, Cantonese and Korean.

When he was 2 years old, Jun's parents divorced and he moved in with his mother. Then when he was 8 years old, Mrs. Jun remarried and had one half-brother. The funny thing is, Jun sometimes sleeps with his eyes open which of course makes the other members feel scared if they happen to pass by. Jun's role models are two Super Junior M members, namely Zhoumi and Henry, because he also wants to receive a lot of support from China and Korea like the two senior idols.

HOSHI

DANCER

  • Full name: Kwon Soonyoung
  • Stage Name: Hoshi
  • Place, date of birth: June 15 1996 in Gyeonggi, South Korea
  • Position in the group: Performance Unit, Dancer
  • Nationality: Korean

Hoshi alias Kwon Soonyoung was born on June 15 1996 in Gyeonggi, South Korea and is also the leader of this performance unit. In the group, he plays as main dancer, lead vocalist and sub-rapper. Hoshi is also known as 10.10 O'Clock because his eyes seem to show the clock hand 10.10.

In 2021, Hoshi released his song entitled "Spider" and received a positive response from fans.

WONWOO

RAPPER

  • Full name: Jeon Wonwoo
  • Stage Name: Wonwoo
  • Place, date of birth: July 17, 1996 in Changwon, Gyeongsangnam-do
  • Position in the group: Hip Hop Unit, Rapper
  • Nationality: Korean

Wonwoo aka Jeon Wonwoo was born on July 17, 1996 in Changwon, Gyeongsangnam-do. In the Seventeen group, Wonwoo has the position of rapper and sub-vocalist. He has been a trainee since 2011 and has also appeared in his seniors' music videos, namely NU'EST entitled "Face" and Hello Venus entitled "Venus".

Wonwoo is an alumnus of the School of Performing Arts Seoul (SOPA) and is currently continuing his education at the Hanyang University Institute for Future Talents majoring in Practical Music KPop Division. During the Weekly Idol program, Wonwoo introduced himself as the member with the lowest votes in Seventeen. Wonwoo also often wears glasses or contact lenses because his eyesight is not good.

Wonwoo admitted that he auditioned for Pledis Entertainment just to gain experience, but instead he passed the audition and successfully debuted with Seventeen. Actually, Wonwoo really wants to be a singer, but people often say that his low voice is suitable for being a rapper. Since then, he has learned how to become a rapper. Last April, Wonwoo's mother died due to illness, this made all the members and CARAT mourn.

WOOZI

VOCAL

  • Full name: Lee Ji Hoon
  • Stage Name: Woozi
  • Place, date of birth: November 22, 1996 in Busan, South Korea
  • Position in the group: Vocal Unit, Vocalist
  • Nationality: Korean

Woozi aka Lee Ji Hoon was born on November 22, 1996 in Busan, South Korea. In the group, Woozi plays the position of leader of the vocal team, lead vocalist, and producer. Yep, Woozi always acts as a producer for all Seventeen's songs.

Woozi has been a trainee since 2011 and even almost debuted with S.Coups for the boy group TEMPEST, but it didn't happen. He was also the composer and producer for I.O.I's song "Downpour". Now, Woozi has become a member of KMCA (Korea Music Copyright Association).

At first glance, Woozi's face looks similar to Suga BTS. Woozi was supposed to be in the performance team, but because he wanted to create his own songs, he joined the vocal team. Woozi once collaborated with Chanyeol EXO for the song "Give Me That"

MINGHAO

DANCER

  • Full name: Xu Minghao/Seo Myung Ho
  • Stage Name: The8
  • Place, date of birth: November 7, 1997 in Liaoning, China
  • Position in the group: Performance Unit, Dancer
  • Nationality: Chinese

The8 aka Xu Minghao was born on November 7, 1997 in Liaoning, China. His Korean name is Seo Myung Ho. In the group, he plays as lead dancer, sub-vocalist and sub-rapper. The8 is the only child in his family and has been passionate about street dancing for 6 years in China.

DOKYEOM

VOCAL

  • Full name: Lee Seok Min
  • Stage Name: DK/Dokyeom
  • Place, date of birth: April 6, 1997 in Anyang-si, Gyeonggi-do
  • Position in the group: Vocal Unit, Vocalist
  • Nationality: Korean

DK aka Dokyeom aka Lee Seok Min was born on February 18, 1997 in Gyeonggi-do, South Korea. In the group, DK plays the main vocalist and his singing skills are no joke!
Together with Hoshi and Seungkwan, he formed the sub-unit BOOSEOKSOON.

DK has been a trainee since 2012. When he first became a trainee, DK admitted that he was very scared and lonely, but in fact he was the trainee who got along with others the quickest. DK really likes rock bands and was even in a rock band when he was in middle school. Together with Mingyu, DK is also part of the '97 Line gang.

There is no need to doubt DK's singing ability, because he often takes part in the "King of Masked Singer" competition and managed to reach the 3rd round. Apart from that, DK also performed in the OST for the drama Tempted entitled "Missed Connections" in 2018.

SEUNGKWAN

VOCAL

  • Full name: Boo Seungkwan
  • Stage Name: Seungkwan
  • Place, date of birth: January 16 1998 in Busan, South Korean
  • Position in the group: Vocal Unit, Vocalist
  • Nationality: Korean

Seungkwan alias Boo Seungkwan was born on January 16 1998 in Busan, but has lived on Jeju Island since childhood. In the group, he plays as the main vocalist and face of the group. Yep, Seungkwan is known as the K-Pop Professor because he understands the ins and outs of K-Pop from the first to the fourth generation. Even fans are often surprised because of their interactions with many senior to rookie idols, also known as Social Butterflies.

Seungkwan has been a trainee since 2012. Previously, Seungkwan was also offered to join JYP Entertainment, but he rejected the offer. Since elementary school, Seungkwan has always participated in children's song festivals and never thought about becoming a singer. Then, one of his teachers recorded Seungkwan's performance and uploaded it to the internet, giving Seungkwan the opportunity to audition for Pledis Entertainment.

Seungkwan's good behavior was not only felt by senior and rookie idols, but also journalists. Once, Seungkwan distributed tangerines to journalists at 5 am without anyone asking. Therefore, Seungkwan is always hailed as a good role model, especially for teenagers. He even won an award in the Rookie Entertainer Award category in 2018.

VERNON

RAPPER

  • Full name: Hansol Vernon Chwe
  • Stage Name: Vernon
  • Place, date of birth: February 18, 1998 in New York, US

  • Nationality: Korean-American

Vernon aka Hansol Vernon Chwe was born on February 18, 1998 in New York, US. Yep, Vernon is one of the foreign members who has Korean-American citizenship. Vernon also has a collaboration group consisting of his close friends called M.O.L.A. (Make Our Lives Awesome) consisting of himself, Jimin (soloist), Seungyoun (WOODZ), and Nathan for the song entitled "Chillin". Even the M.O.L.A group often plays together. Vernon is also good friends with PENTAGON's Kino.

Because he often uses Korean, he feels that his Korean language skills are actually better than English.
Vernon received the casting from Pledis Entertainment staff when he was playing at an internet cafe with his friends. This was revealed in an interview with Japanese Seventeen Magazine. Vernon also took part in a survival show called Show Me The Money 4, but was eliminated in the third round.

DINO

DANCER

  • Full name: Lee Chan
  • Stage Name: Dino
  • Place, date of birth: February 11, 1999 in Jeollabuk-do, South Korea
  • Position in the group: Performance Unit, Dancer

Dino alias Lee Chan was born on February 11, 1999 in Jeollabuk-do, South Korea and is also the Maknae. In the group, Dino plays as the main dancer and sub-rapper. Since he is part of the performance unit, Dino always takes part in creating the choreography, for example for the songs Jam Jam and Flower. Dino really likes Michael Jackson and once appeared in his senior's music video, Orange Caramel, for the song "Copycat".
