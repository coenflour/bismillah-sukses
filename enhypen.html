Groups of Hybe

ENHYPEN

1,2,3, Connect!

ENHYPEN is a group formed through the survival show event entitled I-LAND. ENHYPEN members were the participants with the highest votes at the event. This event was broadcast by Mnet and is the result of collaboration between Big Hit Entertainment and CJ E&M. The two of them agreed to form a new company called Belift Lab to become ENHYPEN's home.

On November 30 2020, ENHYPEN members made their debut by releasing their first EP entitled Border: Day One. To date, ENHYPEN has released many popular songs, such as FEVER, Given-Taken, Polaroid Love, Shout Out, and many others.
