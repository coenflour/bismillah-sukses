Hybe Corporation

Hybe Corporation (Korean: 하이브) is a South Korean entertainment company founded in 2005 by Bang Si-hyuk as Big Hit Entertainment. The company operates as a record label, talent agency, music production, event management and concert production, and as a home music publisher. The company has several subsidiaries, including ADOR, Big Hit Music, Source Music, Pledis Entertainment, Belift Lab, and KOZ Entertainment, collectively known as Hybe Labels.
